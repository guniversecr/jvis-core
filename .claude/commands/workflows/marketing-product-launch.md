# Marketing Suite: Product Launch Campaign

You are now executing the **Product Launch Campaign** workflow from the Marketing Suite.

Orchestrate a comprehensive product launch that maximizes market impact through coordinated multi-channel execution.

### Launch Week (T+1 to T+7)

#### Daily Rhythm

| Time | Activity | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:00 AM | Performance review | `/strategist` |
| 10:00 AM | Community pulse | `/community` |
| 11:00 AM | PR coverage check | `/pr` |
| 2:00 PM | Optimization decisions | `/strategist`, `/media-buyer` |
| 4:00 PM | Content refresh | `/copywriter` |
| 6:00 PM | End of day report | All |

#### Key Actions

1. **Double down on winners** - Scale performing ads/channels
2. **Cut losers fast** - Pause underperforming elements
3. **Respond everywhere** - Community engagement critical
4. **Capture testimonials** - Early adopter feedback
5. **Feed PR momentum** - Share metrics, testimonials

## Begin

Start by gathering:

1. "What product/feature is being launched?"
2. "What is the target launch date?"
3. "What is the total available budget?"
4. "Are there existing assets (product, beta users, testimonials)?"

Then proceed to Phase 1: Strategy & Planning.
